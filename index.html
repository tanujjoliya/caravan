About Catering

Impress your guests with 100% Halal, homemade Afghan cuisine from Caravan Kabob House. We bring the authentic flavors of Kabul straight to your event.

Catering Services

we offer catering services for a wide variety of events such as corporate lunches, cocktail parties and wedding receptions. We cater events in a variety of locations across the GTA including Toronto, Brampton, Mississauga, and Milton. To place a catering request, call 905-497-4876.
